Poio

Poio builds Open Source Technology for Language Diversity to support the next 1000 languages on your computer and phone. We believe that technology should assist the renewal of local languages and cultures by allowing people to actively teach, learn, extend, and spread their language in their community. Our aim is to give people the ability to use their mother tongue in everyday electronic communication, no matter where they are or what language they speak.

Visit the forum to join our community: https://forum.poio.eu

Read more about the project on readthedocs: http://poio.readthedocs.org
